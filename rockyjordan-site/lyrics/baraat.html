About This Song

"Baraat" is Rocky Jordan's Telugu wedding celebration rap — a high-energy track that captures the chaos, joy, and camaraderie of an Indian wedding baraat. From setting up the tent and ordering mutton to hitting teenmaar beats and Naagin dance steps, the song is a love letter to Telugu wedding culture. It's designed to be blasted at every Telugu wedding and friend's sangeet night.
