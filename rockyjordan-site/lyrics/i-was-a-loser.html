About This Song

"I Was A Loser" is Rocky Jordan's debut single from Album 12, released March 12, 2026. The track is a raw, self-aware look at the Telugu IT worker's life in America — the spreadsheets that go nowhere, the EMIs that pile up, the relationships that fall apart, and the quiet shame of being the "uncle" to your neighbor's kids while you're still figuring your own life out. The humor lands because the pain is real. The chorus "Chillaidham chillaidham" is both a coping mechanism and a rally cry — let's breathe, let's get through this, let's vibe.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 'I Was A Loser' about?
It's a Telugu rap about the struggles of an NRI (Non-Resident Indian) IT worker in America — the pressure of EMIs, failed relationships, career stagnation, and the feeling of falling behind while everyone around you moves forward. It mixes humor with genuine vulnerability.
Is this the official lyrics page?
Yes. This is the official lyrics page for 'I Was A Loser' by Rocky Jordan, with transliterated Telugu lyrics and English translations.
Where can I stream 'I Was A Loser'?
The song is available on YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music. Links are at the top of this page.
What is Album 12?
Album 12 is Rocky Jordan's project to release 12 Telugu rap singles over 12 months, one on the 12th of every month, starting March 2026. 'I Was A Loser' is the first single.
