About This Song

"Vandemataram" is Rocky Jordan's award-winning Telugu patriotic rap, written in response to the Pulwama attack and India's surgical strikes. The track moves from India's tradition of peace and patience to the breaking point where tolerance becomes strength. Mixing Telugu, Hindi, and English, it's both a tribute to India's resilience and a warning: "Nayi Bhaarat he, Ghar ghus k maarenge" — This is the new India, we'll enter your home and strike. The music video won Best Music Video at the Mumbai Shorts, Delhi Shorts, and Crown Wood International Film Festivals, plus Best Solo Song at the World Film Festival Los Angeles.
