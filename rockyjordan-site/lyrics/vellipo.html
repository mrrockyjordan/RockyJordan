About This Song

"Vellipo" (meaning "Go Away") is Rocky Jordan's Telugu breakup ballad. The song captures the aftermath of a love that promised forever but delivered betrayal — the memories that won't fade, the stars you search for her face in, and the painful acceptance that some love simply doesn't come back. It's one of Rocky's more melodic tracks, showing the emotional depth beneath the rap bravado.
