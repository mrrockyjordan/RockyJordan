About This Song

Rocky Jordan's Telugu rap version of the viral Sri Lankan hit "Manike Mage Hithe" transforms the romantic melody into a Telugu love poem. While the original captured hearts in Sinhala, Rocky's version weaves Telugu romantic imagery — comparing his beloved to the moon, promising to fight time itself for her, and declaring he'd want to be reborn just to love her again. It's the track that introduced many listeners to Rocky Jordan's lyrical versatility.
