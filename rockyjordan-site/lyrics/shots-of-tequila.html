About This Song

"Shots of Tequila" is Rocky Jordan's Telugu party anthem — a high-energy track about letting go of worries, hitting the club with friends, and living for the night. Mixing Telugu, Hindi, and English, the song captures the immigrant party culture of late-night celebrations where tomorrow's problems can wait. It's the lighter side of Rocky's catalog — proof that the same artist who writes philosophical Granthika can also make you dance.
